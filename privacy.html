Privacy Policy

What data AI Ollama Helper collects, how analytics works, affiliate link disclosure and your choices. Last updated: June 2026.

About this site

Who we are

AI Ollama Helper is a community-driven guide site that helps Windows users download, install and configure Ollama. This site is not affiliated with the official Ollama project or Ollama Inc. The official Ollama website is ollama.com.

Data collection

What we collect

By default we collect no personal data. If you click Accept in the cookie banner, we load Google Analytics 4 to understand site usage. We also store your consent choice in your browser's local storage.

  • Consent choice stored in localStorage as ollama_consent. This is a local value on your device only — we never see it.
  • If analytics is accepted: page views and download button clicks are tracked via GA4.
  • We do not collect: names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any personally identifiable information.
Cookies

Cookies and local storage

This site uses your browser's localStorage to remember your analytics consent preference. We do not set any marketing cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party cookies unless you explicitly accept analytics.

If you accepted analytics and want to withdraw consent: open your browser's developer tools (F12) → Application → Local Storage → delete the ollama_consent key. The cookie banner will reappear on your next visit.

Analytics

Google Analytics 4

If you click Accept, we load Google Analytics 4 (GA4) with consent mode enabled. GA4 is governed by Google's privacy policies. We use it only to understand which pages are useful and how users navigate the site.

  • What we track: page views, session counts, approximate country, and download button clicks.
  • We do not use GA4 for advertising, remarketing, or cross-site tracking.
  • If you click Decline, no analytics scripts are loaded at all.
Affiliate links

Download links and affiliate disclosure

Some download links on this site are affiliate or partner links. We may receive a small commission if you download software through these links, at no extra cost to you. This helps fund the site's operation.

The official Ollama installer is always available for free directly at ollama.com/download/windows.

Third-party links

External links

This site links to third-party websites including ollama.com, GitHub, AMD, NVIDIA and Microsoft. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. Review their policies when visiting external sites.

Your rights

Your choices

  • Accept or decline analytics via the cookie banner on your first visit.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by clearing ollama_consent from localStorage.
  • Use the site with analytics declined — all content is fully accessible without accepting cookies.
Updates

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. The last-modified date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly.
